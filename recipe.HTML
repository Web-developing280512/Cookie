Chocolate cookie recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour: The base of your dough.
  • Sugar: Adds sweetness (usually a mix of white and brown sugar).
  • Butter: Gives flavor and richness.
  • Eggs: Help bind everything together.
  • Baking Soda: Helps the cookies rise and become fluffy.

Instructions

  1. Cream the butter and sugars: In a bowl, beat together softened butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla: Mix in the eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Combine dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together flour and baking soda.
  4. Mix everything together: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and mix until just combined.
  5. Bake: Scoop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
